STEP 2: Build Your Public Narrative

Crafting your public narrative allows you to share your storyâ€”whether in a group setting or a one-on-one conversation. While you may not always have time to tell your full story, you should find ways to weave elements of it into your presentation naturally.

Public narrative is a powerful framework that helps leaders communicate their values and inspire action. It consists of three key parts:

  • Story of Self: Why are you called to do this work? What personal experiences led you to care about voter registration in healthcare?
  • Story of Us: What values and experiences does your audience share? How does your story connect to the broader healthcare community?
  • Story of Now: What urgent challenge threatens these values? What action must we take together?
