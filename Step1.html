STEP 1: Get These 5 Key Takeaways Across

Before moving forward, make sure you're familiar with these key ideas that define our work. Understanding them will help you feel confident in sharing Vot-ER’s mission.

Vot-ER is strictly nonpartisan—we help people register to vote but never tell them how to vote.

Voter registration should be effortless. In under 2 minutes, patients and colleagues can check their voter registration using your Vot-ER badge or Healthy Democracy Kit.

A healthier democracy is one that votes. Policies shape healthcare access, funding, and public health. More voter participation means stronger representation in decisions that impact health.

Major health organizations—including the American Medical Association (AMA)—stand behind nonpartisan voter registration in healthcare.

Healthcare professionals are among the most trusted messengers. You can make a difference by encouraging voter participation in your community.
